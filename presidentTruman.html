About President Harry S. Truman

Harry S. Truman was an American politician who served as the 33rd President of the United States, assuming the office upon the death of Franklin D. Roosevelt during the waning months of World War II.

Here are some of his accomplishments:

  • Truman Doctrine Announced
  • Marshall Plan Announced
  • Desegregation of Armed Forces

President Truman carried out some controversial actions during his tenure. Here are a few such actions in chronological order:

  1. Hiroshima Bombed
  2. Dismissed General MacArthur
  3. Seized Control of the Steel Industry
